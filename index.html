Project Overview

OmniaGene is more than a laboratory; it is a pact with the territory. Located in the heart of Salento, we apply the One Health principle. Currently, Apulia suffers from a lack of advanced diagnostic infrastructure. We reverse this trend by collaborating directly with local hospitals and veterinarians.

The Reality

Patients don't always travel, but their biological samples do. Thousands of samples are shipped North (Milan, Bologna) or abroad for analysis.

Impact on Cancer Care

Delays of 4 weeks to 6 months reduce the chances of remission. Early detection is the #1 factor in cancer survival rates.
