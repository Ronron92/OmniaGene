Bridging the Gap

Project Overview

OmniaGene is more than a laboratory; it is a pact with the territory. Located in the heart of Salento, we apply the One Health principle: acknowledging that the health of humans, animals, and the environment is inextricably linked.

Currently, Apulia suffers from a lack of advanced diagnostic infrastructure, forcing biological samples to travel North for analysis. We reverse this trend by collaborating directly with local hospitals, municipalities, and veterinarians. Our goal is to empower local professionals with world-class genomic intelligence, keeping expertise and value within the region.

The Reality

Patients don't always travel, but their biological samples do. Thousands of samples are shipped North (Milan, Bologna) or abroad for analysis.

Impact on Cancer Care

Delays of 4 weeks to 6 months reduce the chances of remission. Early detection is the #1 factor in cancer survival rates.
